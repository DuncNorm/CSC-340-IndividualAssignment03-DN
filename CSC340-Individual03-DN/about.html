About Us

Pet Cartel is a testament to the power of family dedication and innovation. As a family-owned and operated business, they have embarked on an inspiring journey of growth and evolution over the past five years. What started as a modest venture in a small building has blossomed into a professional grooming haven that sets the standard for excellence in pet care. Their commitment to innovation has been nothing short of remarkable, with the introduction of cutting-edge services such as their mobile pet grooming options, which have brought convenience and quality directly to their customers' doorsteps. Pet Cartel's transformation reflects their unwavering dedication to providing the best possible grooming experience for pets and their owners, making them a cherished institution in the community.

About Our Services

Pet Cartel is the epitome of excellence when it comes to grooming services for animals. Their expertise transcends species boundaries, ensuring that all types of animals receive top-notch grooming and pampering. Whether it's a fluffy dog, a majestic cat, a sleek rabbit, or even more exotic pets like parrots or reptiles, Pet Cartel's skilled team possesses the knowledge and gentle touch to cater to every creature's unique needs. With a commitment to the well-being and comfort of each furry, feathered, or scaly friend, Pet Cartel's grooming services are truly exceptional, leaving pets looking and feeling their very best. When it comes to grooming, Pet Cartel is the go-to destination for pet owners who want nothing but the finest care for their beloved animal companions.
