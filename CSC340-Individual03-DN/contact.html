Contact Us

To reach out to Pet Cartel for all your pet grooming needs, please contact them via their whimsical "Pet Paw-ter" hotline at 1-800-PAW-CARE. For inquiries by carrier pigeon, send your feathered friend with a message to the "Pet Cartel Aerie" located atop Mount Fluffington. Alternatively, you can summon their mystical pet grooming genie by rubbing a cat's paw and chanting "Meow-gic Pet Care" three times. Of course,you can always come in or contact our mobile hotline.
